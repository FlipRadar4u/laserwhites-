Common questions

Frequently asked
questions

How do I book an appointment?

The easiest way to book is via WhatsApp — message us at 07711 253413 and we'll confirm your appointment as soon as possible. We're open Monday to Saturday, 9am to 8pm.

Do I need a patch test before my treatment?

Some treatments — including lash tints, brow tints, and skin peels — require a patch test at least 24 hours before your appointment. We'll let you know if one is needed when you book.

Where are you based and is there parking?

We're on Calveley Avenue, Eastham, Wirral, CH62 9DS. There is free street parking available nearby.

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday, 9am to 8pm. We are closed on Sundays.

How long do treatments take?

Treatment times vary — a lash tint takes around 30 minutes, while a full aesthetic procedure can take up to 90 minutes. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and bank transfer only. We do not accept card payments.
