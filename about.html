Our story

Built on care,
skill & confidence

Welcome to Laser Whites Beauty & Aesthetics — your local premium beauty destination in Eastham, Wirral. We offer a wide range of treatments from everyday beauty essentials to advanced aesthetic procedures, all delivered with the same care and attention to detail.

Whether you're coming in for a quick lash tint or a full aesthetic consultation, you'll always leave feeling refreshed, confident, and taken care of. Our priority is making every client feel comfortable and at ease from the moment they walk through the door.

With over 10 years of experience and more than 3,000 treatments carried out, we've built a reputation on the Wirral for quality, professionalism, and genuine results. We're proud to serve clients from Eastham, Bebington, Bromborough, Heswall, Birkenhead, and across Merseyside.
